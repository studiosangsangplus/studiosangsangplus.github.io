Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to PlantLog on Android and PlantLog Watering on iOS, collectively referred to as “the App.”

The App is a plant watering tracker provided by SangsangPlus. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Store

The App allows users to add plants, set watering cycles, and record watering history.

The following information may be stored on your device:

  • Plant names
  • Plant icons
  • Watering cycle settings
  • Watering records
  • App settings

This information is stored locally on your device. We do not require users to create an account, and we do not operate our own server to collect or store this information.

2. Advertising

The App may display advertisements using Google AdMob or Google Mobile Ads.

Google and its partners may collect or use certain information, such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, app interaction data, and approximate location information, to provide, maintain, and improve advertising services.

For more information about how Google uses information from apps that use Google services, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

3. Notifications

The App may send local notifications to remind you when it is time to water your plants. Notification settings are managed by your device operating system. You can disable notifications at any time through your device settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Depending on your device and platform, the App may use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads
  • Google Play Services (Android)

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not transmit plant names, watering records, or app settings to our own servers. However, third-party services such as Google AdMob may collect or process certain information according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Plant and watering data is stored locally on your device. You can delete plant data within the App by removing plants or watering records. You can also remove all App data by uninstalling the App or clearing App data through your device settings, where supported by your operating system.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page together with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or PlantLog’s privacy practices, please contact us:
studio.sangsangplus@gmail.com
