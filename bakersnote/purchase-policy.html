Purchase & Refund Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Purchase & Refund Policy applies to Baker's Note, provided by SangsangPlus.

1. Baker's Note Plus

Baker's Note may offer a one-time in-app purchase called Baker's Note Plus. Baker's Note Plus unlocks unlimited recipe registration and removes ads.

2. One-Time Purchase

Baker's Note Plus is currently provided as a one-time in-app purchase, not a subscription. After purchase, the premium features are made available according to the app store account and platform where the purchase was completed.

3. Payment Processing

Payments are processed by the app store where you made the purchase, such as Apple App Store or Google Play. SangsangPlus does not directly collect or store your payment card information.

4. Refunds

Refunds are handled according to the refund policy and process of the app store where the purchase was made.

  • For purchases made through Apple App Store, refund requests are handled by Apple.
  • For purchases made through Google Play, refund requests are handled according to Google Play's refund policies.

5. Restoring Purchases

If you reinstall the app or change devices, you may be able to restore your purchase using the same app store account used for the original purchase, depending on platform support and app store rules.

6. Changes to Premium Features

SangsangPlus may improve, modify, or update Baker's Note Plus features over time. Any major changes will be made with the goal of maintaining or improving the value of the purchased premium features.
