Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Baker's Note is provided by SangsangPlus. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Baker's Note mobile application.

1. Information You Enter in the App

Baker's Note allows you to save bread recipes, ingredients, baking steps, categories, notes, and related images. This information is stored on your device and is used to provide the app's core recipe notebook features.

SangsangPlus does not operate a user account system for Baker's Note and does not provide a server-based recipe sync service.

2. Photos and Images

If you add images to your recipes, those images are used only for displaying and managing your recipes inside the app. The app may request access to your device's photo library or image picker only when you choose to add an image.

3. Advertising

Baker's Note may display ads to free users. Advertising may be provided through third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

These advertising services may process information such as device information, advertising identifiers, app activity, or approximate location depending on your device settings, consent choices, region, and the advertising provider's policies.

4. In-App Purchases

Baker's Note may offer a one-time in-app purchase called Baker's Note Plus. Payments are processed by the app store where you made the purchase, such as Apple App Store or Google Play. SangsangPlus does not directly collect or store your payment card information.

5. Third-Party Services

Baker's Note may use third-party services to support app functionality, advertising, purchase processing, crash diagnostics, or platform operation. These services may process information according to their own privacy policies.

  • Apple App Store
  • Google Play
  • Google AdMob

6. Data Sharing

SangsangPlus does not sell your personal information. Information may be processed by third-party services only as needed to provide app functionality, advertising, purchase processing, diagnostics, or legal compliance.

7. Children's Privacy

Baker's Note is not specifically directed to children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can review the issue.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

Recipe data saved in Baker's Note is generally stored on your device. You can delete recipes inside the app, or remove app data by deleting the app from your device.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact
